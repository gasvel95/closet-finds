Closet Finds Privacy Policy

Closet Finds helps users create outfits with AI using the clothing photos and items they upload to the app.

Information We Store

We store account information, uploaded clothing images, outfit preferences, and app activity only as needed to provide the Closet Finds experience.

All information is stored using secure methods designed to protect your data.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only to power app features, including organizing your closet and generating outfit ideas with AI.

We do not share your personal information, uploaded items, or outfit data with anyone.

AI Interactions

Your interactions with AI in Closet Finds are used only to provide responses and outfit suggestions inside the app.

AI prompts, responses, and related content are not used to train models and are not shared for commercial purposes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the Closet Finds team through the app's support channel.
